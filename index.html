Networks of Wellington City

Social Media in Wellington City

The mark social media has on our everyday lives is evident as a thriving network within Wellington City as well.

A sign on the window of Te Auaha advertising the school. It includes a link to their website and several social media badges to indicate their presence on those sites.

They have a badge for Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.

A decal on the window of Cotton On on Cuba Street. They list their website and several social media badges.

The badges shown include Instagram, Pinterest, and others.

A billboard on Church Street for Securapark. It lists their website address below their name and logo.

The hanging sign for (habit) outside of their entrance. It indicates their web address below their logo.

Manhole Covers in Wellington City

There are many manholes in the Wellington Central area. The designs on their covers generally indicate their owners.

A manhole cover from the Wellington City Council. It features a circular logo with a grid of extruded squares.

An alternate design of the same manhole cover, using a hexagon shape for the logo rather than the circle.

A manhole cover belonging to Hynds Pipe Systems. The design is several concentric semi-circles with the Hynds logo in the center.

Another Wellington City Council manhole cover. It has a much more decorative, somewhat floral design.

Wellington City Bus Network

Wellington has a large network of Bus Stops dotted all over the central city.

A digital list of arrival times for the Manners Street Bus Stop, outside Kiwibank. These indicate the scheduled time of arrival for every bus that stops at that stop.

There are many like it dotted all around the city.
